About Orpurkel Farms Ltd

Located in Imbirikani (Kajiado South), Orpurkel Farms combines modern greenhouse horticulture with a small scalable dairy and composting system. We prioritise product consistency, food safety, and sustainability.

  • Location: Imbirikani, Kajiado South
  • Land & Water: reliable piped water and protective soils
  • Markets: Nairobi institutional buyers, hotels, retailers

Greenhouse Program

We run two greenhouse cycles per year and use certified seeds, nursery hygiene, pruning, sticky traps and fertigation guided by lab tests to ensure shelf life and taste.

  • Controlled irrigation & fertigation
  • Grading and cool-holding before dispatch
  • Pest management with IPM

Dairy Program

A scalable dairy with routine health checks, mastitis prevention, chilled collection, and simple value-added options (yogurt).

  • AI breeding & feed optimisation
  • Milk handling aligned to KDB standards
  • Chilled delivery available

Compost & Soil Health

We compost manures and crop residues in windrows, monitor moisture and temperature, and use compost to improve soil structure and water retention.

  • Target ~30 tons/year
  • Batch logs for quality (moisture, pH)
  • Surplus sold locally
