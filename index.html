About Orpurkel Farms

         

Orpurkel Farms, named after the Maasai word for ‘wilderness,’ taps into local water resources to grow premium produce in Kajiado South’s semi-arid land. We blend tradition with modern farming to create a sustainable model for the future.

         

With high-tech greenhouses, a healthy Holstein-Friesian dairy herd, and regenerative composting, we run a circular system that restores the land and supports the community.

         
               
  • Location: Landi area, Imbirikani, Kajiado South, Kenya
    •            
  • Scale: 3 greenhouses, 50+ Holstein-Friesian cows, 10ha fields
    •            
  • Markets: Hotels, schools, retailers
    •            
  • Practices: Drip irrigation, biological pest control, compost (2-1-1 NPK)

Our Sustainable Story

           

At Orpurkel Farms, sustainability is at the heart of our mission. We proudly tap into the Nolturesh River, a vital water source flowing from the slopes of Mount Kilimanjaro, to nourish our crops and livestock in the semi-arid lands of Imbirikani, Kajiado South.

           

Through a pipeline spur extending from the Nolturesh Pipeline to the Chyulu Hills, we ensure efficient water delivery to our high-tech greenhouses and fields, fostering a thriving, eco-conscious farming system. By blending traditional Maasai wisdom with modern regenerative practices, we cultivate abundance while preserving the land for future generations.
