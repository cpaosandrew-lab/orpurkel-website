About Orpurkel Farms

Orpurkel (meaning "wilderness" in Maasai) is a modern agricultural enterprise in Imbirikani. With access to the Nolturesh Water Pipeline, we cultivate greenhouse vegetables, manage an ethical dairy herd, and produce quality compost — all while investing in local jobs and skills.

  • Location: Imbirikani, Kajiado South
  • Scale: 2 modern greenhouses, open fields, dairy unit
  • Markets: Nairobi hotels, restaurants, schools, retailers
  • Practices: IPM, fertigation, compost-based fertilization

What Makes Us Different

Traceability, ethical livestock care, disciplined record-keeping, and a commitment to soil health. We partner with local communities to create value and improve livelihoods.

Our Sustainable Story

We combine modern greenhouse systems with community-centred regenerative agriculture. Below are the pillars of our approach.

Greenhouse Excellence

Our greenhouses are designed for climate control, efficient water use and integrated pest management. This ensures superior tomato and capsicum yields year-round.

  • Climate control & shade management
  • Automated fertigation (micro-dosing)
  • Biological pest control and IPM

From Compost to Crops

We convert dairy manure and crop residues into rich compost that feeds our soils, reduces reliance on synthetic fertilizers, and improves water retention.

Compost is tested periodically to ensure nutrient balance and safety.

Ethical Dairy Farming

Our Friesian and Ayrshire herd is managed with humane practices, balanced feeding (hydroponic fodder where applicable), and regular veterinary care to ensure milk quality and animal welfare.
