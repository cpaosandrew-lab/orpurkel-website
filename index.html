About Orpurkel Farms

Orpurkel (meaning "wilderness" in Maasai) transforms the semi-arid lands of Imbirikani into a hub of sustainable agriculture. Leveraging the Nolturesh Water Pipeline, we operate modern greenhouses, manage a high-yield Friesian dairy herd, and produce nutrient-rich compost to enrich soils.

  • Location: Imbirikani, Kajiado South, Kenya
  • Operations: 3 advanced greenhouses, 10ha open fields, 50+ Friesian cows
  • Markets: Nairobi hotels, restaurants, schools, and retailers
  • Techniques: Fertigation, IPM, regenerative composting, hydroponic fodder

Our Commitment

We prioritize traceability, ethical livestock management, and soil health. By training and employing local talent, we foster community resilience and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Sustainable Story

We blend cutting-edge agricultural technology with regenerative practices to produce food sustainably and empower the Imbirikani community.

Greenhouse Innovation

Our three greenhouses use automated fertigation, climate control, and biological pest management to produce high-yield tomatoes and capsicum year-round.

  • Drip irrigation for 80% water efficiency
  • IPM with beneficial insects (e.g., ladybugs)
  • Polycarbonate roofing for optimal light diffusion

Regenerative Composting

We produce over 40 tons of compost annually from dairy manure and crop residues, enriching soils and reducing chemical fertilizer use by 60%.

Our compost is lab-tested for nutrient content (NPK) and safety.

Community Empowerment

We employ and train over 25 locals in modern farming techniques, provide seedlings to smallholder farmers, and run workshops on sustainable agriculture.

Our buy-back program supports local growers by purchasing their produce for our supply chain.
