About Orpurkel Farms

Named after the Maasai word for "wilderness," Orpurkel Farms harnesses the Nolturesh Water Pipeline to cultivate premium produce in Kajiado South’s semi-arid landscape. Our state-of-the-art greenhouses, high-yield Friesian dairy herd, and regenerative compost operations drive sustainable agriculture and local empowerment.

  • Location: Simba Cement, Within Imbirikani Group Ranch, Kajiado South, Kenya
  • Scale: greenhouses, Holstein-Friesian cows,
  • Markets: hotels, schools, retailers
  • Practices: Drip irrigation (80% water efficiency), biological pest control, compost with 2-1-1 NPK

Our Mission

To deliver traceable, premium produce while restoring soils, conserving water, and creating sustainable livelihoods for the Imbirikani community.

Our Sustainable Journey

We blend modern technology with regenerative practices to produce sustainable food and empower the Imbirikani community.

Greenhouse Excellence

Our three greenhouses use drip irrigation (80% water efficiency), ladybug-based IPM, and polycarbonate roofing to grow Roma VF tomatoes and capsicum year-round.

  • Automated fertigation for precise nutrient delivery
  • Biological pest control reduces chemical use by 70%
  • Polycarbonate roofing optimizes light diffusion

Regenerative Compost

We produce 40 tons of compost annually (2-1-1 NPK) from dairy manure and crop residues, reducing chemical fertilizer use by 60% and improving soil water retention.

Community Impact

We train 100+ local farmers annually in sustainable techniques, provide seedlings, and run a buy-back program to integrate smallholder produce into our supply chain.
