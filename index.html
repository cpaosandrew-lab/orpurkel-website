Our Sustainable Story

Greenhouse Excellence

Our two modern greenhouses are the heart of our vegetable production. Here, we meticulously control environmental factors, ensuring our tomatoes and capsicum are grown under optimal conditions, free from common pests and diseases. We use an integrated pest management (IPM) system to reduce chemical use, relying on natural predators and barriers.

From Compost to Crops

Regenerative agriculture is central to our philosophy. We use manure from our dairy cows, combined with crop residues, to create nutrient-rich compost. This natural fertilizer enriches our soil, improves water retention, and reduces our dependency on chemical inputs, leading to healthier, more flavorful produce.

Ethical Dairy Farming

Our dairy cows are a key part of our sustainable ecosystem. Managed with care and ethical practices, they provide us with a consistent supply of high-quality milk. We optimize their feed with hydroponic fodder, ensuring their health and maximizing milk yield while using minimal water.
