About Orpurkel Farms Ltd

Greenhouse Program

Our two state-of-the-art greenhouses in Imbirikani operate year-round, utilizing certified seeds, precision fertigation, and integrated pest management (IPM) to produce premium tomatoes and capsicum with exceptional taste and shelf life.

  • Controlled drip irrigation & fertigation
  • Rigorous grading and cool-holding
  • IPM with sticky traps and minimal chemicals
  • Two cycles per year for consistent supply
Request a Greenhouse Tour

Dairy Program

Our scalable dairy operation features Friesian and Ayrshire cows, managed with AI breeding, optimized feed, and strict hygiene protocols. We deliver chilled milk and value-added yogurt, compliant with Kenya Dairy Board (KDB) standards.

  • AI breeding for high-yield genetics
  • Feed optimization with hydroponic fodder
  • Chilled delivery to Nairobi and beyond
  • Mastitis prevention and routine health checks

Compost & Soil Health

Our regenerative compost program transforms manure and crop residues into nutrient-rich compost, enhancing soil structure and water retention. Surplus compost is sold locally to support Kajiado’s farming community.

  • Target production: ~30 tons/year
  • Monitored windrows (moisture, pH, temperature)
  • Supports local soil health initiatives
  • Reduces chemical fertilizer dependency
